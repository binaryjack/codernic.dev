# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026  
**Operator:** The Codernic Team (Switzerland)  
**Contact:** [Contact Form](https://codernic.dev/contact)

This Privacy Policy explains how the Codernic Team collects, uses, and protects your data when you use the Codernic software, website, and related services. This document is designed to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and relevant Asian data protection frameworks (e.g., PDPA, APPI).

---

## 1. Local-First & On-Premise Philosophy
The fundamental architecture of Codernic is **Local-First**. 
- **Your Code is Yours:** The Codernic VS Code Extension and the local Codernic Daemon process your codebase, Abstract Syntax Trees (ASTs), and proprietary logic entirely on your local machine.
- **No Phoning Home:** We do not upload, sync, or harvest your source code, workspace structures, or intellectual property to any Codernic-controlled servers. 
- **Enterprise Isolation:** If you utilize Codernic Enterprise, all B2B microservices run within your own corporate infrastructure.

## 2. What Data We Collect & Where It Lives
While the core engine is strictly local, interacting with our public website, documentation wiki, or licensing services may result in the collection of minimal data:
- **Usage & Analytics:** Anonymous telemetry (e.g., page views on this wiki) strictly used for improving documentation.
- **Contact Information:** If you use our contact form or email us, we collect the details you provide (e.g., email address) solely to respond to your inquiry.
- **Licensing Data:** If utilizing premium features, we may validate license keys via a secure, one-way cryptographic check that records the activation IP and timestamp.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Swiss Datacenter Sovereignty:** Any data that is collected (such as licenses or contact information) is stored **securely in our own datacenters located exclusively in Switzerland**. No data is entrusted to, stored by, or processed by any third-party cloud provider.

## 3. Global Compliance Disclaimers

### A. European Union (GDPR) & Switzerland (FADP)
You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us. Because we do not store your local codebase data, such requests generally only apply to emails or contact inquiries you have sent us. To exercise your rights, email `[Contact Form](https://codernic.dev/contact)`.

### B. United States (CCPA/CPRA)
**Do Not Sell My Personal Information:** The Codernic Team does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties under any circumstances. 

### C. Asian Markets & International Data Transfers
Codernic does not mandate cross-border data transfers of your proprietary codebase. Any local processing remains within your sovereign jurisdiction. If you choose to connect Codernic to third-party Cloud LLMs (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic), you are responsible for ensuring those APIs comply with your local data sovereignty laws. We recommend utilizing Codernic's **Pirsig Shield** (AST Anonymizer) to prevent PII leakage before data is transmitted to external LLMs.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Codernic integrates with third-party LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI, Azure, Anthropic). When you route prompts to these providers, their respective privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for the data handling practices of third-party model hosts.

## 5. Changes to this Policy
As we transition from our pre-incorporation "Try-up" phase into a registered commercial entity, this Privacy Policy will be updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
