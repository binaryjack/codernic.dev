# Non-Disclosure Agreement (Beta Testers)

**Parties:** The Codernic Team (Switzerland) and the Beta Tester.

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("NDA") governs the exchange of proprietary information during the Beta testing phase of Codernic, pursuant to the freedom of contract principle under **Art. 19 of the Swiss Code of Obligations (CO)** ([admin.ch RS 220, Art. 19](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/27/317_321_377/en#art_19)).

## 1. Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to, unreleased software features, AST parsing logic, local daemon architectures, and UI designs. The Beta Tester agrees to keep all such information strictly confidential.

## 2. Obligation of Confidentiality
The Beta Tester shall not reverse engineer, decompile, or publicly share screenshots, code snippets, or workflows of the Codernic Beta without prior written consent from the Codernic Team.

## 3. Breach and Remedies (Penalty Clause)
To foster mutual trust without discouraging adoption, we rely on a proportional penalty clause. In the event of a deliberate and proven breach of this NDA (e.g., publishing proprietary source code), the Beta Tester shall be liable for the reparation of the exact damages suffered by the Codernic Team. This clause is governed by **Art. 160 CO (Penalty Contract)** ([admin.ch RS 220, Art. 160](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/27/317_321_377/en#art_160)), rather than an arbitrary fixed monetary fine, ensuring fair and proportionate accountability.

## 4. Governing Law
This agreement is governed by Swiss law. Any dispute shall be resolved in the competent courts of Switzerland.
