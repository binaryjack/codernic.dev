# Enterprise Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Provider:** The Codernic Team (Switzerland)

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") outlines the performance metrics, support availability, and liability parameters for Codernic Enterprise deployments.

## 1. Nature of the Service
The provision of support and enterprise maintenance is legally structured as a Mandate under **Art. 394 of the Swiss Code of Obligations (CO)** ([admin.ch RS 220, Art. 394](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/27/317_321_377/en#art_394)). The Codernic Team pledges to act with due diligence and care in maintaining the software.

## 2. Uptime and Response Times
- **Critical Issues:** 24-hour response time.
- **General Support:** 48-hour response time during standard Swiss business hours.
- *Note: As a "Try-up" (pre-incorporation) entity, enterprise SLA metrics are provided on a best-effort basis until commercial incorporation is finalized.*

## 3. Limitation of Liability
Pursuant to **Art. 100 CO** ([admin.ch RS 220, Art. 100](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/27/317_321_377/en#art_100)), the Codernic Team legally and explicitly excludes any liability for damages arising from **light negligence** (négligence légère). Liability is retained solely for unlawful intent (dol) or gross negligence (faute grave), as mandated by Swiss federal law. 

Under no circumstances shall the Codernic Team be liable for indirect damages, loss of profits, or data corruption resulting from the autonomous actions of connected LLMs.

## 4. Governing Law
Governed exclusively by Swiss law.
