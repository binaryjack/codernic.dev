# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (Enterprise)

**Parties:** The Codernic Team (Switzerland) and the Enterprise Client.

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("NDA") is established to protect the proprietary trade secrets of both parties during B2B negotiations and technical evaluations, in accordance with the freedom of contract (**Art. 19 CO**) ([admin.ch RS 220, Art. 19](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/27/317_321_377/en#art_19)).

## 1. Mutual Confidentiality
Both parties may exchange sensitive information. For the Enterprise, this may include internal infrastructure details and proprietary code. For Codernic, this includes enterprise deployment architecture, B2B SDKs, and proprietary AST anonymization rules.

## 2. Duty of Loyalty and Secrecy
In alignment with the principles underlying **Art. 398 CO** ([admin.ch RS 220, Art. 398](https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/eli/cc/27/317_321_377/en#art_398)) and **Art. 162 of the Swiss Criminal Code** (Violation of manufacturing or trade secrets), both parties pledge absolute loyalty and secrecy regarding the exchanged Confidential Information.

## 3. Local-First Guarantee
The Codernic Team explicitly guarantees that its evaluation software operates purely locally. No Enterprise codebase data will be exfiltrated to Codernic servers.

## 4. Term and Governing Law
The obligations of confidentiality shall survive the termination of negotiations for a period of 5 years. This agreement is exclusively governed by Swiss law.
