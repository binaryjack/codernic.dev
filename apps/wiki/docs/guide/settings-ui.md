# Settings & UI Widgets

Codernic features a flexible, drag-and-drop dashboard designed to let you orchestrate your AI environment effortlessly.

## 1. The Workspace Layout

The Codernic UI is completely customizable. You can drag, drop, and resize different panels (widgets) to arrange your workspace perfectly to your liking.

* **Visual Settings Editor:** A user-friendly interface that lets you click toggles and dropdowns to configure your project, rather than forcing you to write complex JSON code.

## 2. Global vs. Project Settings

Codernic separates your personal preferences from your team's rules:

1. **Global Settings**
   These are settings tied to your specific laptop, such as whether to use your graphics card for faster AI processing, or your preferred color theme.
2. **Project Settings (`.codernic` folder)**
   These settings live directly inside your project folder. They manage project-specific rules, such as which files the AI should ignore and what framework standards to follow. If you share your project, your team gets these same settings automatically.

## 3. Managing Teams & Workflows

Using the **Agents Dashboard**:
* **Agents:** You can create specialized AI teammates (e.g., a "Security Reviewer" or a "Database Architect") simply by typing out what you want their job to be.
* **Visual Workflows:** You can draw maps connecting your agents together. For instance, you can link a "Planner" agent to three parallel "Coder" agents, and funnel their work into a "Reviewer" agent.

You can manage all of these visually through the UI dashboard without needing to touch any code files.
