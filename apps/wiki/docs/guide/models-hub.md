# Choosing Your AI Brain (Models Hub)

Codernic gives you the freedom to choose what "brain" powers your AI. You can run models entirely on your own machine for ultimate privacy, or connect to powerful cloud APIs.

## 1. Local Brains (The Models Hub)

The **Models Hub** is your one-click destination for downloading AI brains that run directly on your laptop.

1. Open the **Models Hub Panel** in the Codernic UI.
2. Browse recommended AI brains (e.g., Llama-3, Qwen).
3. Click **Download**. Codernic handles all the complex fetching behind the scenes.
4. Once downloaded, select the model to start using it immediately.

> [!NOTE]
> Codernic automatically detects if you have a powerful graphics card (GPU) and uses it to make the AI respond faster. You don't need to configure any hardware settings yourself.

## 2. Cloud Brains

If you prefer to use powerful commercial AI like OpenAI (GPT-4) or Anthropic (Claude), you can securely connect them to Codernic.

1. Navigate to the **Settings Widget** -> **Models**.
2. Paste your API key into the secure input box.
3. The keys are encrypted and stored safely on your machine.

## 3. Smart Brain Switching

In **Enterprise Edition**, you can enable Smart Switching. Codernic will automatically evaluate how hard your question is.
* Trivial tasks (e.g., "fix this typo") are sent to your fast, free local AI.
* Complex architectural tasks are automatically routed to a massive cloud AI.
This maximizes speed and saves you money without you ever needing to manually swap models.
